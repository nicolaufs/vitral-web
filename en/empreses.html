From Vitral Vidre we offer the best glazing solutions to the current needs of the construction sector, both in the residential field, as well as in rehabilitation, commercial or industrial.

Our main activities are:

  • Cutting and manufacturing of large monolithic and laminated glass.
  • Manufacture of Double and Triple Insulating Glass under the brands SGG CLIMALIT and SGG CLIMALIT PLUS
  • Laminated Glass
  • Tempered Glass
  • Curved Glass
  • Fireproof Glass

Some of the products we offer:

  • Safety Glass - SGG STADIP
  • Acoustic Glass - SGG STADIP SILENCE
  • Thermal Insulation Glass - SGG CLIMALIT · SGG CLIMALIT PLUS
  • Low E glass - SGG PLANITHERM XN
  • Solar Control Glass - SGG PLANITHERM COOL-LITE
  • Selective Glass - SGG PLANITHERM 4S · SGG PLANISTAR ONE · SGG COOL-LITE XTREME · SGG COOL-LITE SKN

The management of construction projects consists of the definition and determination of the documentation and planning of the technical requirements and the characteristics of the work to be executed.
This planning is the basis on which the daily work of site technicians and is divided into:

  • Project study and analysis
  • Budget preparation
  • Documentation and work planning
  • Manufacturing
  • Installation

At Vitral Edificació we carry out projects for new construction, rehabilitation, public works, office spaces, industrial buildings and commercial buildings.
