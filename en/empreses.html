Management of Construction Projects

The management of construction projects consists of the definition and determination of the documentation and planning of the technical requirements and the characteristics of the work to be executed.
1.

Project study and analysis i Análisis del proyect

Initial evaluation and detailed analysis to determine the project's feasibility.

2.

Budget Preparation

Calculation of anticipated costs to ensure appropriate execution.

3.

Documentation and work planning

Preparation of all necessary documents and detailed schematics to guide the project's execution.

4.

Manufacturing

Production and assembly process following performance specifications, design, and quality.

5.

Installation

Implementation and final assembly at the construction site, ensuring compliance with established plans and standards.

At Vitral Edificació we carry out projects for new construction, rehabilitation, public works, office spaces, industrial buildings and commercial buildings
