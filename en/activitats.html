Construction Glass

At VITRAL GRUP we offer glazing solutions to the current needs of the construction sector. We work in the residential building field, in rehabilitation, we do commercial spaces and also industrial plants.

Our main activities are:

Technical Advice

The regulatory framework that regulates the characteristics that different materials for building must have is the Technical Building Code or CTE.
Regarding enclosures, whether made in aluminum or in PVC, they must meet certain requirements such as:

  • Thermal transmittance
  • Air permeability
  • Condensation control
  • Solar factor

All of these requirements vary regarding the geographical area and orientation where the installation is made. The territory is divided according to the different climatic conditions which directly condition the usability of the different carpentry profiles and, based on the results of tests carried out in specialized laboratories, the suitability and compliance of the requirements in each area are determined.
All VITRAL GRUP carpentry meets all the requirements to perform installations in any geographical point.
Furthermore, we provide advice and calculation services such as:

  • Thermal Transmittance calculations of windows
  • Inertia calculations according to closure dimensions and installation location
  • Calculation of required acoustic attenuation, according to acoustic maps of cities
  • Calculation of air flows for renovation and application of aeration systems
  • Application of safety regulations in glazed enclosures
