WHAT WE DO?

BUILDING GLASS · CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS · ALUMINIUM AND PVC ENCLOSURES · ALUMINIUM & ACCESSORIES

BUILDING GLASS

Solar protection glazing, low emissivity, thermal and acoustic insulation, privacy control, safety glass, glass for interiors, screen-printed glass, special shapes.

Construction Projects

Planning, technical support, manufacturing and installation in the process and execution of architectural projects either new work, rehabilitation, public works, industrial and commercial buildings, projects with high thermal requirements and acoustic...

Aluminium & PVC Enclosures

We manufacture Aluminum and PVC enclosures. With the VIT LINE line we offer a wide range of products for practicable, sliding, elevating windows and doors. If you are an installer, contact us.

Profiles and Accessories for Aluminium Enclosures

Commercialization of aluminum systems and accessories for the manufacturing of all types of closures for new construction and renovation of architectural spaces.

We create value in each process by offering the most advanced, technical and aesthetic constructive solutions with the most professional and humane treatment.

WHY VITRAL GRUP?

Trajectory

We are a family company created in 1962 dedicated to the glass manufacturing and distribution. In the 70's we introduced the manufacture and commercialization of aluminum carpentry and, since the last decade, we have incorporated in our product catalog a new line of PVC enclosures, currently offering a complete, human-centered business culture which strives to excel in quality.

Work Ethic

At Vitral Grup we respond to the technical, aesthetic, architectural, and regulatory requirements in the construction sector, evolving according to the most avant-garde trends. Our work is strengthened by a honest and high quality human service, with a team of professionals and experts, who respond to the highest technical standards and are proud of their work. This familiar business culture, coupled with the social and environmental commitments we have maintained since our founding, have allowed us to grow gradually with rigor and prudence as professionals within the sector.

Human Team

It’s our great employees who make the Vitral Grup project possible: The management team, the technicians, the sales team, the logistics department and the different production teams are made up of great people who are committed to their work, which makes them essential to offer the service that our clients and the sector deserves.
