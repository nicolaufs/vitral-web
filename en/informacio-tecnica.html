Guarantees

Guarantees SGG CLIMALIT® & SGG CLIMALIT PLUS®

All SGG CLIMALIT® and SGG CLIAMLIT PLUS® glazings come with the VITRAL GRUP warranty for a period of 10 years from the date of manufacture.

What does the warranty cover?

The warranty covers all manufacturing defects that manifest in the form of stains on the inside of the double or triple glazing, caused by moisture condensation or dust deposits.

All of this, provided that:

The SGG CLIMALIT® / SGG CLIMALIT PLUS® pieces have been correctly mounted in an appropriate carpentry.
The installation of the windows has respected the specifications and the work placement standards, collected in the official regulations or in the Saint-Gobain Glass Manual.
The regular maintenance of the window has been adequate. The lack of cleaning or inadequate maintenance could cause obstruction of the drainage in the carpentry glazing and damage the double glazing. These damages will not be covered by this warranty.
