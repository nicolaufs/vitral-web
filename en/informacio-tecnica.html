Warranties

Warranties SGG CLIMALIT® & SGG CLIMALIT PLUS®

All SGG CLIMALIT® and SGG CLIAMLIT PLUS® glasses have the VITRAL GRUP warranty for a period of 10 years from the date of manufacturing.

What does the warranty cover?

It covers all manufacturing defects that manifest as stains on the inside of the double or triple glazing chamber, caused by condensation of humidity or by dust deposit.

All this, as long as:

The SGG CLIMALIT® / SGG CLIMALIT PLUS® parts have been assembled correctly in a suitable carpentry.
In the installation of the windows, the specifications and the norms of installation contained in the official regulations or in the Saint-Gobain Glass Manual have been respected.
Regular window maintenance has been adequate. Lack of cleaning or improper maintenance could cause obstruction of the drainage in the joinery of the carpentry and damage the double glazing. These damages will not be covered by this warranty.

Product manual and tolerances.

VITRAL GRUP® manufactured enclosures Warranty

All the closings, whether in aluminum or pvc, and other manufactured products by VITRAL GRUP such as blinds, mosquito nets, fences, railings... have a warranty of 2 years from the date of manufacture.

What does the warranty cover?

It covers all those manufacturing defects that affect the product aesthetics and / or functionality.
