El Garrofer School

The El Garrofer public school in Viladecans, Barcelona, ​​has become the first to carry out a full energy rehabilitation in Spain. The project, which aims to obtain the Passivhaus / EnerPhit seal, is the first work of its kind.

VITRAL installed nearly 300 large SYNGEO tilt-and-turn windows, with SGG CLIMALIT PLUS double glazing and a controlled double-flow ventilation system, 5 double emergency exit doors, all bicoloured, with white interior and anthracite exterior.

  • Client: Novantia
  • Date: 2018
  • Location: Viladecans, Barcelona
  • Product tag: #syngeo #double glazing #rehau #insulation #passivhaus #largedimensions #cliamlitplus #enerphit

Balneari Caldea

The prestigious French architect Jean-Michel Ruols, known as the architect of water, managed to provide the Caldea building with a unique and harmonious image with mineral and crystalline references. It is a building in which straight and vertical lines predominate, as well as dynamic forms inspired by Andorran nature, mountainous, which calls for activity and fun.

Vitral Grup manufactured and supplied the exterior glazing for the new glass building with, mainly, tempered 8mm Ipasol Bright Bronze glass.

  • Client: Caldea
  • Date: 2012
  • Location: Esclades-Engordany, Principat d'Andorra
  • Product tag: #solarcontrol #othermalinsulation #confort #LargeDimensions #design #temperate
